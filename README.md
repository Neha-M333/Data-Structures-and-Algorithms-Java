#### 1. Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock
Find the maximum profit from a single buy-sell transaction.
- **Time Complexity:** `O(n)`
- **Space Complexity:** `O(1)`
- **Approach:** Single-pass tracking of minimum price

#### 2. Trapping Rain Water
Calculate total water trapped between elevation bars.
- **Time Complexity:** `O(n)`
- **Space Complexity:** `O(n)`
- **Approach:** Precompute left and right maximums
